## Online Samples
ArticleExpress was an editing tool for scientific journal editors. After the articles were received and formatted for publishing, editors could perform 
tasks on the articles. Each journal had its own needs, so we produced different versions of the software for each. As such, we needed to produce 
multiple versions of the online documentation from a single source. We used MadCap Flare to accomplish this and served the software and the Help 
from Amazon servers.

Here is the [Online Help for The Journal of Pediatrics](/store/Online/Pediatrics/PFXHelp.htm){:target="_blank"}.
