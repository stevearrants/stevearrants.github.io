How to run maintenance programs

Agilent instruments invariably have recommended maintenance programs built into their basecode and installed on your computer during LHC installation.

Click Open to open the Liquid Handling Control version#> Protocols folder to find maintenance protocols for your instrument.

Otherwise, refer to the "Maintenance" chapter in the user manual for further guidance.
