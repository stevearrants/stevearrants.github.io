Resize the screen

The LHC offers a few ways to resize its screens to improve legibility or to suit your preferences:

  • The bottom-right corner of most screens offers the resize handle: click and drag to desired size and shape.
  • The main view and Transfer Protocols screen offer internal resizing. Hover the cursor between the two boxes: Protocol Steps and Step Details on the main screen, and This computer and Instrument on the Transfer screen, until it becomes a two-headed pointer, then click and drag to desired size.
