Delay Control

This control opens when a Delay step is reached during a run.

Click Continue when you're ready to end the delay and proceed with the Protocol.

FYI
  • Fixed Duration and Timer Activated Delay: the washer/dispenser will resume processing when the time expires or when the Continue button is pressed. The amount of time remaining to end the delay is shown in the title bar, as it counts down to 0.
  • Indefinite Delay: the washer/dispenser will not resume processing until the Continue button is pressed. The duration of the delay is shown at the top of the window.
  • Prompt: the person who defined the protocol wrote the text in the Prompt box. He/she expected any directions to be followed before proceeding with the run.
