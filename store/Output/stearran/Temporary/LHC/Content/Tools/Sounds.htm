Sounds

Tools> Preference> Sounds

This LHC control lets you customize the alarm sounds triggered by a Reminder and a Delay step:

  • Reminders are the user-defined tasks or events. The alarm rings when it is scheduled to occur and at startup when an unresolved reminder is past due.
  • Delays are user-defined protocol steps. The alarm will ring, when enabled, at the start of the delay.

Click the links to:

  • Select a sound file. The LHC opens Windows® default folder for storing .wav files.
  • Click Play to preview (listen to) the selected sound file.
  • Reset to restore selection of the default sound files.
