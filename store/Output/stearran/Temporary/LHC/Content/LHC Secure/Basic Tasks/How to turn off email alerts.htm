Turn off email alerts

LHC Secure: Only a system administrator is permitted to change this setting.

LHC Secure can deliver email alerts to designated users when certain events occur.

To turn off email alerts:
  1. Select System>Security>Settings.

  1. Select the Email Alerts tab and clear the checkbox. (Click the box to toggle it on or off.) This disables all email alerts.
To turn off or disable one alert:

To discontinue sending one type of event and keep sending alerts for the remaining types: click the first cell in the table row to select it, highlighting the entire row, and press the Delete key on your keyboard.

To delete multiple rows simultaneously, press the Shift or Ctrl keys during selection, then press Delete.
