Change your password

For LHC Secure only.

System> Security> Users

Users other than the System Administrator are limited to changing their own login password.

How to change your password:
  1. Select System> Security> Users.
  2. Enter your new password in both the Password and Confirm Password fields.
  3. Click Save.

The password will take effect the next time you log into the LHC.

Contact your System Administrator if you do not remember your password. They can change your password without knowing the current one.
