Contact Technical Support

The Liquid Handling Control (LHC) software is backed by a superior support staff. If the software fails to function properly, please contact Technical Support. You can call, write, fax, or email your questions and concerns to Agilent.

Whichever method of contact you choose, please be prepared to provide this information:

  • The LHC software version displayed at Help> About Liquid Handling Control.
  • The instrument's Serial Number
  • The instrument's software versions: Select Tools> Instrument Utilities> Software to display them on-screen.
  • The specific steps that produce your problem.
  • Any error codes displayed.
  • A daytime phone number.
  • Your name and company information.
  • An email address, if available.
