Accessing Your Article

You can access your article to make edits any time before you click Submit. The only exception is when a CollaboratorA colleague to whom you give permission to perform edits, commenting, or other tasks on your article. is working on the article, as only one user at a time can edit the article. During those times you can use a read-only version of your article. Once you submit your article, a read-only version will be available to you for 30 days.

To access your article
  1. Open the email you received from the Production Editor or Corresponding Author.
  2. Click the link in the email. ArticleExpress opens the article.

Keep this email. You cannot access the article without the link in the email. You can add the link to your browser's Bookmarks.
