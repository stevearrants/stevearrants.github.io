Insert Section

Production Editors can use this command to insert a new section in your article, such as Corrected Data.

To add a section
  1. Place the cursor where you want the new section to appear.
  2. Click the Insert Section button . ArticleExpress inserts the Title placeholder, formatted in light gray text.
  3. Place your cursor immediately before Title and type the new text. You do not have to select the word Title. ArticleExpress replaces the placeholder with your text.
