(AR) Author Corrections

Refer to your specific Workflow and process documentation for more information.

Summary:

After the eProof task closes, the proofs are out to the author for corrections.

Full Description:

The author is reviewing the article and making corrections. When she is finished she can return the article via TAC or send them to the PE.

With TAC
  1. The Author Center sends a call to (Undefined variable: MyVariables.ProductA) with the data and location of the files
  2. (Undefined variable: MyVariables.ProductA) retrieves the file or files from the specified location and uploads them to the task.
  3. (Undefined variable: MyVariables.ProductA) closes (AR) Author Corrections task and opens the (AR)Corrections Review task.
Without TAC
  1. As PE, contact the author and have her send the files via FTP or another method.
  2. In the ((AR) Author Corrections task, click Edit. (Undefined variable: MyVariables.ProductA) automatically assigns the task to you and locks the task.
  3. Using the File Manager, upload all files to the task.
  4. After all files have been uploaded, click Complete. (Undefined variable: MyVariables.ProductA) closes the (AR) Author Corrections task and opens the (AR)Corrections Review task.
