Inserting and Deleting Text

Place the cursor where you want to insert text and begin typing. You can also paste text, To delete text, use the Backspace key or select text and press Delete.

Note: Some sections won't allow you to insert or delete text. For example, you can't directly type text into the Affiliations area. Use the Insert Footnote>Affiliation command or insert a comment to the Production Editor.
In other cases, your role within ArticleExpress may limit which areas are available for you to edit. If you need to insert or delete text and cannot, insert a comment with instructions to your Production Editor.
