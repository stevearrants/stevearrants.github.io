Roles in ArticleExpress

What you can do—in both editing and reviewing—is determined by the role you've been assigned. Some of the commands and functions described in Help may not be available to you due to your configuration or your role within ArticleExpress.

Here is a brief overview of roles in ArticleExpress:

  • Production Editor, Publisher: Access to all commands and functions.
  • Corresponding Author: Access to all commands and functions except the Edit Metadata, Tools, and Processes menus.
  • Collaborators have four different levels of access:
    1. Full Proxy: The same rights as the Corresponding Author, including inviting other collaborators and submitting the article.
    2. Editing and Query Response: The collaborator can make edits and respond to queries.
    3. Editing Only: The collaborator can make edits and add comments.
    4. Comment Only: The collaborator can only comment with the Comments widget or the Comments toolbar button.

If you think your role is wrong

What can you do if your assigned role is wrong or doesn't allow you to do certain tasks? First, check the original email you received. It lists your role for this article. If you need access to other tools, contact the Corresponding Author or the Production Editor for assistance. Remember that some actions, such as adding images and tables, can only be performed by a Production Editor.
