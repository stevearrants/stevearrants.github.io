Author Queries

Before you, as author, can submit the article, you must respond to all queries in the Author Queries widget.

Click a numbered tab, and ArticleExpress takes you to that query.

You can respond in one of three ways:

  • Yes/OK—You agree with the editor and accept the editor's solution.
  • No—You do not accept the editor's solution and have one of your own, entered in the comments below the query.
  • See Text Edits—You've edited the text in question, and the editor will review the edits.
