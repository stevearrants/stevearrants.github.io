Interface and Commands

When you log into ARTEMIS Dashboard, the first screen you see is the Article Dashboard. Search filters are at the top and the ArticleDashboard showing matching results is underneath.

Dashboard Commands

When finished with your work, click Log Out, in the upper-right corner of your screen.

Dashboard Interface

The Page arrows at the bottom of the Article Dashboard allow you to move from one page to the other. Click the right or left arrows to move forward or back through the list of tasks. Click the down arrow to adjust the number of results ARTEMIS Dashboard displays on the Tasks screen.
