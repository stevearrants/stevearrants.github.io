How do I add issues to my journal titles?

NOTE:Note

You must get the job numbers from Teresa before you start.

To add issues to your journal titles
  1. Click Production>Titles.
  2. Select your journal and then click View.
  3. On the Title Details screen, expand the Issues section.
  4. In the Issues section, click Add.
  5. ARTEMIS displays the Issue section.
  6. Add the requested information. When you add the numbers for Volume and Issue, don't include the letters v or i. For Name, we recommend the volume and issue numbers with the v and i included. For example, for volume 23 and issue 12, type v23i12.
  7. When complete, click Save. ARTEMIS adds the new issue and returns you to the Issue Details screen for the new issue.
