Where do I show that Data Supplements have arrived for an article in production?

  1. Select any task for that article record and view the task.
  2. In the upper right corner, click the underlined article ID.
    ARTEMIS opens the ArticleAn Article may be a review, editorial, and other component that makes up an Issue. Details record.
  3. Edit the record and click the check mark in the Additional Information section, Data Supplements field.
