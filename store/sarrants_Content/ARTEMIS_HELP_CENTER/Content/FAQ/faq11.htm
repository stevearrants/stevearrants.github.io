Where can I find workflow charts?

You can find the ARTEMIS workflow charts at J:\Transition_Planning\Artemis\Implementation

Open the folder with your customer acronym. If there are workflows, they will be in a WorkflowThe sequence of process through which an ad, article, issue, or front and back mater pass from creation to completion. or Workflows folder.
