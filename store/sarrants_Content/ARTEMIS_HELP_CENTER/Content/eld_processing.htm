MCDS Delivery and the ELD Processing Task

NOTE:Note

This feature is undergoing development and enhancement.

When ARTEMIS sends the signal to TNQ to start creating the ELD files for upload to the online host, it opens the ELD Processing task. The Signal Ack. Date on this task shows that TNQ received the task. When the files are uploaded and TNQ completes their QC, the task closes, and ARTEMIS displays a Complete Date on ELD Processing task. This completes delivery of the files to MCDS for the mobile app.

 

Further development coming soon, including making the MCDS confirmation more visible in ARTEMIS.
