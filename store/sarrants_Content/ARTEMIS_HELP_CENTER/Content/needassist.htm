If You Have Questions, See Errors, or Need Changes

If you have any questions, see errors, or need changes made to other programs, please follow the instructions below.

Questions

If you have an ARTEMIS question, contact Susan Clark.

If you have an ArticleExpress question, enter a JIRA Service Desk ticket and don’t forget to enter your name.
Errors

If you have an ARTEMIS error, enter a JIRA Service Desk ticket and don’t forget to enter your name.

If you have an Author Center or eBill error for an ARTEMIS title, enter a JIRA Service Desk ticket and don’t forget to enter your name.

If you have an ArticleExpresserror, enter a JIRA Service Desk ticket and don’t forget to enter your name.
Changes If you have changes that need to be made to EditExpress, eGalley, FreeStyle, Author Center, eBill, or Validator, please enter a TrackIt work order.

When in doubt, contact Susan Clark via email, Skype, phone at extension 329 or 802-882-1618, or stop by her desk.

Please let us know if you have any questions or concerns.
