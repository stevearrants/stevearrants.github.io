(AR) Copyediting

Refer to your specific Workflow and process documentation for account-specific information.

Summary:

The manuscript is with the Copy Editor, who performs a copy edit prior to (AR) Composition.

Full Description:

If your workflow has a (AR)Freestyle QC task, open the email and review it. Then go to the next step.

  1. Edit this task.
  2. Put the files on an FTP location for the Copy Editor to retrieve them.
  3. Assign the task to the Copy Editor.
  4. Click Notify. ARTEMIS sends an email to the Copy Editor that the files are available. When finished, the Copy Editor returns the files to the PA.
  5. As PA, upload the files
  6. In the Additional Info section, type the page count next to CE Pages.
  7. At Figure Corrections, select Y, N, or R.
  8. Click Complete. ARTEMIS closes the task.

Related Tasks:

Depending on your workflow, one or more of these tasks may or may not be part of the workflow.
