(FMBM) Pull an Article Out to Allow for Two Page TOC

  1. In an (IS) task, go to the Issue Sequencing section.
  2. Select the article by clicking it.
  3. Unassign the article from the issue.
  4. Recalculate the pages.
  5. Confirm results.
