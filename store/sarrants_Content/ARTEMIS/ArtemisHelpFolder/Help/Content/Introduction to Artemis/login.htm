Your ARTEMIS Account

Logging In

ARTEMIS does not use your standard Windows log in credentials. Instead, use the ARTEMIS credentials you've been assigned.

The URLs for ARTEMIS are:

  • Training site: http://uat.artemis.dartmouthjournals.com/ARTEMIS/Pages/Login.aspx
  • Live site: http://live.artemis.dartmouthjournals.com/Artemis/Pages/Login.aspx

Forgot your Password?

ARTEMIS uses a different password than the one you use for Windows, Outlook, and other network resources. If you forget your password, click the Forgot your password? link and a new password will be sent to your email address.
