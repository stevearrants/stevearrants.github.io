How do I Turn Off Reminders?

Sometimes you want to turn off theARTEMIS Reminders—sometimes called "nag emails"—for your articles. You do this from the root display of the component. Here's how to turn them off.

To turn off Reminders
  1. Click View on any task of an article.
  2. In the breadcrumbs, click the Article ID.

    ARTEMIS displays the Article Details screen. The Article Details screen is also called the root display.
  3. Click Edit.
  4. In the Additional Information section, select the Disable Reminders check box. ARTEMIS disables all reminders for this article.
  5. Click Save.
